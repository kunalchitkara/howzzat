Workflow 4 · Billing

Post-match charging

20p per player when you finalize

  • Charge triggers on match finalize, not at schedule time
  • 20p × players in confirmed lineup (both teams)
  • Lineup locked at toss / squad confirm — that's the player count
  • Example: 4v4 U9 pairs → 8 players → £1.60 per match
  • Clubs on a free trial are not charged until the trial ends
